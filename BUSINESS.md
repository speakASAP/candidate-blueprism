# Business

## Vision

Provide a simple web-based candidate exercise that lets an automation candidate express process design clearly without needing local tooling or Blue Prism installed.

## Goal Impact

- Assess whether a candidate can model a business process with clear structure.
- Capture the candidate's assumptions, open questions, and optimization thinking.
- Keep the exercise lightweight to run, review, and deploy.

## Current feature scope

- A palette of flowchart shapes relevant to process mapping.
- A drag-and-drop editing surface for nodes and connections.
- Inline text editing and removal of shapes.
- Export of the created diagram from the browser.
- Static hosting through Kubernetes for interviewer/candidate access.

## Constraints

- This is an assessment tool, not a production automation runtime.
- The UI should remain simple and dependable over feature-rich.
- Assessment content must stay generic unless the owner explicitly changes the brief.
